Privacy Policy

White Care Nursing Recruitment ("us", "we", or "our") operates the website and is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal information we collect and how it is used.

We adhere to the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) outlined in the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) to govern the collection, use, disclosure, storage, security, and disposal of your Personal Information. These principles dictate how we handle your data with utmost care and respect.

For a detailed reference, you can access a copy of the Australian Privacy Principles on The Office of the Australian Information Commissioner's website at www.aoic.gov.au.

Information Collection and Use: We may collect personal information from you, including but not limited to your name, email address, phone number, and professional qualifications. This information is used solely for the purpose of nursing recruitment and to communicate with you about potential job opportunities.

Information Sharing: We do not share your personal information with third parties unless required by law or with your explicit consent. Your information will only be disclosed to potential employers as part of the recruitment process.

Security: We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access or disclosure. However, please note that no data transmission over the internet or storage can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

Cookie Policy: We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our website. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Links to Third-Party Websites: Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of such websites. Please review the privacy policies of those third parties before providing any personal information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy: We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the date of the latest revision will be indicated at the top of the policy.

Contact Us: If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@whitecarenursing.com.au
